About Us

Express Rest is the most preferred hotel in West Africa and it's all about the right mind. Located in the heart of Victoria Island and shielded from the hustle and bustle of the Lagos metropolis, we offer corporate clients and walk in guests a perfect blend of relaxation, activities and African tradition delicately infused to meet the highest international standard.

Overlooking the Eko Atlantic City Centre and Ocean, it is just a 10-minute drive to the City centre and only 45-minutes away from thr Airport. Our hotels are designed for your comfort and convenience. Your security is our primary concern and you'll find our customer care second to none.

Meet the CEO

Mark Essien (born December 18, 1990 in Ikot Ekpene, Akwa Ibom State) is a Nigerian entreprerneur, software developer and startup investor. He is the founder and CEO of Hotels.ng, one of the first online hotel booking websites in Nigeria. Before founding Hotels.ng, Mark Essien had previosly built a file sharing software called Gnumm (acquired by Snoopstar.com), followed by a language learning startup called Ingolingo.

In 2015, the comapny secured additional investment funding of $1.2m from international investment funding of $1.2m from international investors EschoVC Pan-Africa Fund, [6] a seed -stage technology fund, and Omidyar Network, the investment vehicle of eBay founder Pierre Omidyar.
